NMhelper Privacy policy

Your data

Request data deletion

School staff and school contacts can ask us to delete NMhelper accounts and related data stored on our servers.

What we can delete

Depending on your request, we remove data from our servers, including:

  • Head or teacher account (name, username, hashed password)
  • Teachers and classes linked to a school head account
  • Class strength records (daily boys/girls counts and related history)
  • School settings stored for that account (for example class limits)

We do not store student names or photos. Deleting an account does not remove files you already saved on your own device (for example exported reports). Uninstalling the app clears local app storage on your phone.

Who can request deletion

  • The signed-in user (head or teacher) for their own account
  • A school head for their school’s data (including teacher accounts they manage)
  • An authorized school contact (for example principal or office) on behalf of the school

If you are not sure which username to quote, include your school name and your role — we will confirm before deleting anything.

How to request deletion

Email us from an address your school recognizes. Include:

  • School / institution name
  • Your role (head, teacher, or school contact)
  • NMhelper username (and display name if known)
  • What to delete (single teacher, whole school head account, etc.)

We may reply once to verify you are authorized. We aim to complete verified requests within 30 days.
