Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

1. Introduction

NmHelper is an educational app designed to help schools manage class strength details. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the data we collect.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Class Strength Information (number of boys and girls per class)

No personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location is collected.

3. Who Can Enter Data

Only authorized users—such as Heads of Schools and Teachers—can input class strength data into the app.

4. How We Use the Data

The data collected is used solely for administrative and analytical purposes within the school system. We do not share or sell any information to third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

NmHelper does not collect any personal data from children or students. The data stored is class-level information only.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect the data within the app from unauthorized access.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at nishalamv@gmail.com.
